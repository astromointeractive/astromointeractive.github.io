- Resource scarcity and trade negotiations  
- Crew conflicts and morale management

Cosmic Builder stands out from other management games with its attention to the unique challenges of space-based construction. Every system is interconnected, creating emergent gameplay where small decisions can have far-reaching consequences.

The game rewards both careful planning and adaptive thinking, as you'll need to respond to the unexpected while building toward long-term goals.
