## The Story

In the year 2387, humanity has expanded across the galaxy through a network of ancient alien gateways. As Captain Nova Sterling, you command the exploration vessel *Astromo*, tasked with investigating mysterious signals from the galaxy's unexplored outer rim.

What starts as a routine survey mission quickly becomes something much larger, as you uncover evidence of an ancient civilization and a threat that could reshape the galaxy forever.
