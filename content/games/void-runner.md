## The Experience

Void Runner drops you into the cockpit of a sleek starfighter racing through treacherous void zones filled with asteroids, debris, and other hazards. Your mission is simple: survive as long as possible and rack up the highest score.

But simple doesn't mean easy. As you progress, the challenges intensify:
- Asteroid density increases
- New hazard types appear
- Speed multipliers test your limits
- Power-ups provide temporary advantages

## Game Modes

### Classic Mode
The original endless runner experience. How far can you go before the void claims you?

### Time Attack
Race against the clock to achieve the highest score in a limited time. Perfect for quick gaming sessions.

### Daily Challenges
New challenges every day with special leaderboards and unique rewards.

### Zen Mode
A more relaxed version with checkpoints and no time pressure. Perfect for practice or casual play.

## Power-Ups & Abilities

- **Shield Boost**: Temporary invincibility to plow through obstacles
- **Time Dilation**: Slow down time to navigate tight spaces
- **Score Multiplier**: Double or triple your points for a limited time
- **Magnetic Field**: Attract bonus items and power-ups
- **Phase Shift**: Briefly phase through solid objects
